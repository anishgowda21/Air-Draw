How to Draw
  1. Pinch to Draw: Bring your thumb and index finger together to start drawing.
  2. Release to Stop: Separate your fingers to stop drawing.
  3. Adjust Settings: Use the controls below to change drawing thickness and color.
  4. Distance Slider: Lower value = easier to draw, higher value = more precise.
